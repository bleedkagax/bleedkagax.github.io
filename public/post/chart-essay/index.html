    程度#

    逐渐的#

    slightly modestly gradually

    稳定的#

    steadily sustained

    显著的#

    noticeably remarkably markedly

    巨大的#

    significantly remarkably dramatically rapidly sharply

    大量的/少量的#

    a great/signifcant/major proportion (of…) (only) a fraction (of…)

    最大量/少量#

    greatest/most significant proportion (of…) smallest/least significant proportion (of…)

    以不同的程度#

    to different/varying degrees
